Scholarship Program

At AfroCodersHub, we believe that financial barriers should never stop a motivated individual from accessing world-class tech education. Our scholarship program makes that possible.

Our Commitment

Equal Access to Tech Education

🎯 What We Believe
We're dedicated to providing opportunities for aspiring tech enthusiasts to excel in the ever-evolving world of IT. Through our scholarship program, we offer a diverse range of courses tailored to meet the demands of the modern tech landscape — empowering learners with cutting-edge skills and certifications.

What sets us apart is our commitment to flexibility and accessibility. All our training programs are conducted online, accommodating the schedules of both instructors and students while providing a seamless learning experience.
✨ Apply for Scholarship
🌍
Open to All Backgrounds
Our scholarship is open to motivated individuals from any country, background, or experience level who demonstrate commitment to learning.

100% Online

📅
Flexible Scheduling
Sessions are designed to accommodate working professionals and students with varied schedules across time zones.

Requirements

Scholarship Eligibility

We want to support the most motivated candidates. Here's what we look for:

✅ You Qualify If...
✓
You demonstrate genuine passion for technology and learning
✓
You face financial barriers to accessing quality tech education
✓
You can commit to attending live sessions and completing assignments
✓
You have a reliable internet connection and a device
✓
You are 16 years or older
✓
You are willing to participate in our community and give back
✓
You can communicate in English (basic proficiency sufficient)
📋 What to Prepare
✓
A short personal statement (why you want this scholarship)
✓
Your chosen course from the eligible programs above
✓
Your current employment/student status
✓
How you plan to use the skills after completing the program
📌 Important Note
Scholarship spots are limited each cohort. We review applications on a rolling basis and notify successful candidates within 5–7 business days. Applying early improves your chances!
